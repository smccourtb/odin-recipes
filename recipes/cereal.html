Cereal

Description

Breakfast, lunch, or dinner! This all around versatile dish is sure to please even the most sceptical and picky eaters.

Ingredients

  • 1 box - Your favorite ceral
  • 2 cups - Milk

Steps

  1. Go to your cereal cupboard and select your favorite cereal.
  2. Pour into a bowl.
  3. This step is important because if you skip the previous step and do this step first, you will be widely regarded as a psychopath in society. Pour milk OVER cereal.
  4. Spoon up and enjoy! Keep the box handy!
