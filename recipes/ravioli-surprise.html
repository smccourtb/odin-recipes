Ravioli Suprise

Description

Cheesy baked goodness wrapped up in ravioli! With a bit of sour cream for good measure. (The surprise is brocolli!).

Ingredients

  • 2 cans - Chef Boyardee Ravioli (Family Size)
  • 4 oz - Sour Cream
  • 1 lbs - Shredded Cheese
  • 1 head - Brocolli

Steps

  1. Mix both cans of ravioli with sour cream in large bowl.
  2. Steam brocolli and lay in a casserole dish.
  3. Our ravioli/sourc cream mixture on top of the brocolli, filling the casserole dish
  4. Bake in oven at 425 for 25 minutes or until the edges start bubbling.
  5. Sprinkle the cheese over the entire surface. Evenly.
  6. After cheese is melted. Take it out of the oven, let it cool down, and enjoy!
